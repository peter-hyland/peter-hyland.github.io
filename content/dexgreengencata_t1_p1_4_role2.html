Discrete 3.0mm Cable

Description: Surface-mounted fibre cable suitable for internal wall or riser installations with proper conduit/sub-duct for mechanical protection. Available in G657A2 or G657B3 fibre types, both bending insensitive.

Features: Flame retardant, compatible with DexTidy accessories, standard length of 1000 meters.

Types

Description: Robust and pre-connectorised options available for internal and external environments. Multiple colours and customisation available.

Blown Cable

Description: Designed for Underground installation in telecommunication network infrastructures, offering increased mechanical protection for modular fibre tubes.

LSZH Duct

Description: LSZH microducts ideal for fibre blowing with low friction, designed for indoor installation. Available with internal texturing options; Smooth, Ribbed.
