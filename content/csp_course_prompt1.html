CSP Demarcation Box Installation

Unique Feature of CSP Box Lid: Locks at 45

Primary Function of CSP Demarcation Box: For installation of copper connections

CSP Sealing After Installation: Seals when you close the lid

Mounting CSP to Customer's Wall: Mount using glue

Tool List for Installation: Use any tools you have available

Locating and Managing Underground Services

Suitable Inside/Out Cable Layer for Outside Use: Outer sheathing is UV protected

Tools and Equipment Usage: Inside/Out Cable for managing bend radius

Taking Length into Account for Installation: Length from CSP to ONT

Management of Cable Slack: Inner slack storage

Handling Cable Faults During Testing: Proceed with installation anyway

Traffic Management for Excavation Works

Purpose of Site Investigation: Ensure plans are current

Responsibilities of Asset Owner: Provide up to date plans for proposed work area

Importance of Marking Service Locations: To reduce risk of damage

Personal Safety Risks: Injury from road traffic

Control Measures for Risk Reduction: Use high visibility clothing

Risk Assessment and PPE Determination: Carry out site-specific risk assessment

Backfill Material Classification and Reuse

Classification of Backfill Materials: Class A (granular) to Class E (unsuitable)

Acceptable Backfill Material Characteristics: Suitable for specifications

Reuse and Storage Requirements: Covered and segregated to prevent contamination
