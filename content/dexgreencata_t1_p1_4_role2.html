DexGreen Introduction

Summary: DexGreen, founded in 1994, specializes in developing innovative products for upgrading carrier networks globally. Utilizes AR and AI technology for digital installation assistance and skills training via a mobile learning platform.

Telecommunications Connectivity Products

Summary: DexGreen offers over 200 unique products designed for telecommunications infrastructure. Highlights innovative, cost-effective solutions accredited to quality and environmental standards. The mission is focused on improving network health and reliability.
