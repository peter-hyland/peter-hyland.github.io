CSP Demarcation Box Installation

Unique Feature of CSP Box Lid: Locks at 45

Primary Function of CSP Demarcation Box: For installation of copper connections

Sealing of CSP after Installation: The CSP seals when you close the lid

Mounting CSP to Customer's Wall: Mount the CSP using glue

Management of Inside/Out Cable

Suitable Layer for Outside Use: Only the outer sheathing is UV protected

Equipment for Cable Bend Radius: Inside/Out Cable

Length Consideration for Installation: Only the length from CSP to ONT

Storage of Outgoing Cable Slack: In the inner slack storage

Tool for Stripping Fibre Cable: An open-edged blade

Action for Faulty Cable During Testing: Proceed with installation anyway

Detection and Risk Assessment

Site Investigation Importance: To ensure plans are current

Responsibility for Providing Plans: Asset owner

Marking of Services Location: To reduce the risk of damage

Personal Safety Considerations: Injury from road traffic

Risk Reduction Control Measures: Use of appropriate PPE, traffic management

Traffic Control and Safety

Purpose of Traffic Management Layout: To ensure safe flow of traffic

Traffic Control Equipment: Stop works sign, footway boards, pedestrian barriers with tapping rail

Personal Protective Equipment Importance: To reduce risks from traffic

Safety Zones and Walkway Widths: To protect site operatives and pedestrians

Warning Lights and Signage Requirements: Ensure visibility and compliance with standards

Excavation Safety

Excavation Inspection: Identify high-risk areas

Site Specific Risk Assessments: Identify risks and control measures

Trench Support Equipment: To prevent trench collapse

Street Excavation Equipment: Road saw, road breaker, suction excavator

Backfill Material Classification and Reuse

Classification of Backfill Materials: Class A to Class E

Backfill Material Reuse Criteria: Field identification tests, compliance with standards

Reuse Advantages: Environmentally sustainable, cost-effective

Storage and Segregation: Covered, bunded, separated by class

Disposal of Unsuitable Materials: Licensed recycling site
