Dexgreen Catalogue Summary

How to Create an Account

Description: Require emailing info@skillsbase.io to register an account. Separate registration needed for SkillsBase App.

Note: Operator account credentials are distinct and cannot be used in the SkillsBase App.

Dexgreen Catalogue Summary

How to Sign In

Instructions: Navigate to https://operator.skillsbase.io/ and enter provided login details.

Requirement: An account must be previously created and credentials received via email.

Dexgreen Catalogue Summary

Changing your Password

Steps: After logging in, go to 'My Account' to change the password via 'Change Password' option.
