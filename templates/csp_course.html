CSP Demarcation Box Features and Installation

Unique feature: Locks at 45 degrees

Primary function: Installation of copper connections

Sealing method: Seals when the lid is closed

Mounting: Using glue

Handling and Installation of Inside/Out Cable

Protection layer: Outer sheathing is UV protected

Bend radius management: Inside/Out Cable

Length measurement: From CSP to ONT

Slack storage: In the inner slack storage of CSP

Stripping tool: Open-edged blade

Equipment and Tool Usage

Tool availability: Use any tools available

Cable retention system: DexTidy

Knockout removal tool: Flathead screwdriver

Site Investigation and Planning

Purpose: Ensure plans are current

Action when plans are not current: Carry out a site investigation

Asset owner's responsibility: Provide up-to-date, readable plans

Service and Cable Identification

Colour coding

Low pressure PE gas: Yellow

Water: Blue

Low voltage electricity: Black

High voltage electricity: Red

Intermediate pressure gas mains: Orange

Detection challenges

PE water and gas services: Not detectable by conventional devices

Electric cables: DC current or no flow makes detection difficult

Risk Assessment and Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)

Risk calculation: Likelihood x severity

PPE for road traffic: High visibility clothing

PPE for electrical or gas services: Flame retardant overalls

Damage Prevention and Control Measures

Prevent gas pipe damage: Do not use mechanical excavators within 500mm of a gas pipe

Action upon finding a fault: Proceed with installation or report to asset owner

Detection Equipment Use and Limitations

PUWER: Employer responsibility for equipment suitability

Calibration: Depends on manufacturer's instructions

Traffic Management and Public Safety

Method selection: Based on traffic flow and road speed limit

PPE: Appropriate based on site specific risk assessment

Signage: Follow TSRGD or TSR (NI) standards

Construction and Excavation Practices

Trench support: Used when identified in risk assessment

Excavation categories

Deep excavation: >1.5m depth

Narrow trench: <=300mm width

Material Handling and Reinstatement

Backfill material classification

Class A: Graded granular materials

Class E: Unacceptable materials

Re-use guidelines: Based on compliance with reinstatement classifications
