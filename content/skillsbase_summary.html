CSP Installation

Unique Feature of CSP Box Lid: Locks at 45

Primary Function of CSP Demarcation Box: For installation of copper connections

Sealing of CSP after Installation: CSP seals when you close the lid

Mounting of CSP to Customer's Wall: Mount using glue

Tools for Installation List: Use any tools available

Storage of Outgoing Cable Slack: In inner slack storage

Handling of Faulty Inside/Out Cable: Proceed with installation anyway

Cable Management in CSP: Use DexTidy cable retention system

Inside/Out Cable

Suitability of Inside/Out Cable Layers: Only outer sheathing UV protected for outside use

Equipment for Managing Bend Radius: Inside/Out Cable

Length Measurement for Installation: Length from CSP to ONT

Stripping Tool for Fibre Cable: Open-edged blade

Handling Splices and Faults: Secure with glue/tape, replace cable if needed

Site Investigation and Risk Assessment

Purpose of Site Investigation: Ensure plans are current

Responsibility for Providing Plans: Asset owner

Actions for Ensuring Plans are Current: Check date of issue

Marking Services for Excavation: To reduce risk of damage

Underground Services

Identification of Service Risks: Risk assessment
