How to Create an Account

Instructions: To create an account, email info@skillsbase.io to have one registered in your name.

Note: Operator account credentials cannot be used for the SkillsBase App.

How to Sign In

Instructions: Open your web browser and go to https://operator.skillsbase.io/. Enter the login details sent to your email.

Changing your Password

Recommendation: It is recommended to change your password after logging in. Follow the steps in the 'My Account' section.

Using the Dashboard

Features: Dashboard includes quick view options for Users, Groups, Courses, Modules, Products, and Resources. Sidebar menu for navigation.
