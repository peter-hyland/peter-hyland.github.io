Installation of CSP Demarcation Box

Unique Feature of CSP Box Lid: Locks at 45

Primary Function: For installation of copper connections

Sealing After Installation: CSP seals when lid is closed

Mounting to Customer's Wall: Mount using glue

Tools List: Use any tools available

Copper Connector Cables Routing: Through small 101A hole in rear center of box

Handling of Inside/Out Cables

Suitable Layer for Outside Use: Only outer sheathing is UV protected

Equipment for Bend Radius Management: Inside/Out Cable

Measurement of Cable Length: Only length from CSP to ONT considered

Storage of Outgoing Cable Slack: In inner slack storage

Tool for Stripping Fibre Cable: Open-edged blade

Management of Fibre Cable Splicing

Action in Case of Fault: Proceed with installation anyway

Demarcation Box Cable Retention: DexTidy cable retention system

Grommet Splitting Method: Use any sharp blade

Equipment for Provisioning Knockout Removal: Flathead screwdriver

Knockouts at Rear of CSP: Copper provisioning only

Site Investigation and Risk Assessment

Purpose: Ensure plans are current

Responsibility for Providing Plans: Asset owner

Checking Plans for Currency: Verify date of issue

Marking Service Locations: To reduce risk of damage

Risk Reduction Action for Underground Services: Dig trial holes

Traffic Management and Signage

Purpose: Reduce traffic disruption

Main Risk: Injury from road traffic

Control Measure Determination: Balancing risk against practicality

Suitable PPE for Road Traffic Risks: High visibility clothing

Control Measure for Traffic Safety: Highway traffic management layout

Excavation Safety and Utility Services

Consequences of Utility Damage: Severe burns, explosion, etc.

Response to Gas Main Damage: Contact national gas emergency line

Protection of Electrical Cable: Clear area, contact asset owner

Reporting Damages: Regional water authority for sewer pipe

Utilities Damage Prevention: No mechanical excavators near gas pipes

Backfill Materials Classification

Importance of Material Segregation: Prevent contamination

Protection of Reusable Materials: Covered and stockpiled

Storage of Unsuitable Materials: Segregated within site barriers

Removal of Unsuitable Materials: To licensed recycling site

Implications of Unsuitable Backfill: Reinstatement failure
