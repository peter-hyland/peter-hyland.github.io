Account Creation and Sign In

How to Create an Account: Users need to email info@skillsbase.io to have an account created for the Operator Dashboard.

How to Sign In: After the account is created, users can sign in at https://operator.skillsbase.io/ with the credentials sent to their email.

Password Management

Changing your Password: After logging in, users can change their password by accessing 'My Account' and selecting 'Change Password'.

Dashboard Navigation

Using the Dashboard: The Home Screen provides quick view options like Users, Groups, Courses, Modules, Products, and Resources. The sidebar menu allows navigation to different sections like Courses, Users, Products, and Team Management.

User and Course Management

User Management: View, create, edit, and manage users with options to assign courses, view training progress, and perform actions like account activation or deletion.

Course Management: Create, edit, and manage courses, modules, and submodules with options for customization, publishing, and setting progression criteria.

Submodule Templates

List of Submodule Types: Includes Title Slide, Web Viewer, Video Player (YouTube/Vimeo/Upload), Image Gallery, Itemised List, Do and Don'ts Scenarios, Mini Quiz, Step-by-Step Video Guide, Hotspot Image, Simple Question, Hotspot Uploader, Question Bank Quiz, Upload Media, Checklist, and Document Viewer.
