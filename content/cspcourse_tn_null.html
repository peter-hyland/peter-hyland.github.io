Installation of CSP Box

Unique feature of CSP box lid: Locks at 45 degrees

Primary function of CSP Demarcation Box: For installation of copper connections

Sealing CSP after installation: CSP seals when lid is closed

Mounting CSP to customer's wall: Mount using glue

Tools list for installation: Use any available tools

Out cable slack storage: In inner slack storage

Stripping Inside/Out Fibre Cable: Use an open-edged blade

Inside/Out Cable Management

Layer suitable for outside use: Outer sheathing is UV protected

Equipment for managing cable bend radius: Inside/Out Cable

Measurement of cable length: Only length from CSP to ONT

Where to store outgoing cable slack: In outer slack management clips

Cleaning exposed fibre before splicing: Use any piece of cloth

Site Investigation and Risk Assessment

Purpose of site investigation: To ensure plans are current

Action at planning stage to ensure plans are current: Carry out a site investigation

Responsibility of asset owner for buried services information: Provide up-to-date readable plans

Checking to ensure plans are current: Check date of issue

Marking services location before excavation: To reduce risk of damage

Traffic Control and Signage

Personal protective equipment for traffic work: High visibility clothing

Determining safety exclusion zones: Completing risk assessment

Purpose of temporary traffic control: To reduce risks to site personnel

Setting up signing and guarding activities: Use route-specific risk assessment

Equipment for light vehicle access during excavation: Footway boards

Excavation Safety

Identifying high danger areas in excavation: Complete risk assessment

Inspecting excavations for risk areas: Competent person at the start of shifts

Site-specific risk assessments for excavation: To identify risks and controls

Protecting pedestrians during excavation: High visibility clothing

Backfill Materials Management

Classifying backfill materials: Class A to E

Reusing excavated materials for backfill: When complying with classifications

Segregating reusable materials: To prevent contamination

Stockpiling acceptable materials: Covered to avoid excessive dryness
