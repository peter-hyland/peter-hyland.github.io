How to Create an Account

Procedure: Contact info@skillsbase.io to have an account registered in your name

Note: Operator account credentials cannot be used for the SkillsBase App

How to Sign In

Steps

  • Open web browser
  • Go to https://operator.skillsbase.io/
  • Enter login details received via email

Changing your Password

Steps

  • Click 'My Account' button
  • Select 'Change Password'
  • Update password as required

Using the Dashboard

  • Management options for Users, Courses, Products, Resources, Jobs, News, Reports, Team Members, and Account
