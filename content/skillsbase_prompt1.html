How to Create an Account

Description: To sign into the Operator Dashboard (Expert), it is necessary to have an account. Accounts need to be created manually by contacting info@skillsbase.io.

Key Steps

  • Email info@skillsbase.io to have an account registered in your name.
  • Receive login details after the account is created.

Description: Once an account is created, sign in through the web browser using the provided login details.
