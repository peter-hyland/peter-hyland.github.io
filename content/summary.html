WHO WE ARE

DexGreen was founded in 1994 and develops innovative and tailor-made products to upgrade carrier networks worldwide. They manufacture over 200 unique products and offer ongoing support through their mobile app.

INNOVATIVE AND COST-EFFECTIVE

DexGreen creates innovative and cost-effective telecom products, providing ongoing product support services through technical, commercial, and collaborative relationships with business partners.

ACCREDITATIONS

DexGreen is accredited to BS EN ISO9001:2015 quality standard and BS EN ISO14001:2015 environmental standard, covering design, manufacture, and distribution of products and services to various industries.

OUR MISSION

DexGreen's mission is to improve network health with products and solutions that deliver whole life cost savings through innovation, fault volume reduction, and supply chain refinement, dedicated to improving telecommunications infrastructure.

SUSTAINABILITY

DexGreen is committed to sustainability, reducing single-use plastics, waste, and energy consumption, implementing environmentally friendly practices in manufacturing and operation.

FIBRE TO THE HOME

Offers various products for optical distribution frames, fibre jumper management, patch cords, cables, and more for fibre connectivity in exchange settings.

UNDERGROUND NETWORK

Features various sizes of optical nodes with different capacities for splicing, patching, and splitter configurations suitable for underground deployment.

Provides blown fibre cables with robust construction for underground installations, suitable for blowing or pulling in ducts.

Includes single subducts, triplex subducts, end caps, and gas block connectors for long term protection of fiber optical cables underground.

AERIAL NETWORK

Includes products like NODES and Circular Port Kits for aerial network distribution.

Features various brackets for managing slack cables efficiently in aerial deployments.

Offers versatile pole brackets and extenders for mounting and extending equipment on poles in aerial networks.

Includes brackets and clamps for securing cables and equipment on poles in aerial network installations.

Products for overhead cable management and support in aerial network setups.
