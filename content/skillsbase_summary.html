CSP Installation

Unique feature of CSP box lid: Locks at 45

Primary function of CSP Demarcation Box: For installation of copper connections

Sealing of CSP after installation: The CSP seals when you close the lid

Mounting of CSP to customer's wall: Mount using glue

List of tools needed for installation: Use any available tools

Cable Routing and Splicing

Suitable layer of Inside/Out cable for outside use: Only the outer sheathing is UV protected

Equipment for managing cable bend radius: Inside/Out Cable

Length measurement of Inside/Out cable for installation: Only length from CSP to ONT

Storage of outgoing cable slack in CSP: Inner slack storage

Tool for stripping Inside/Out Fibre Cable: Open-edged blade

Action if fault found in cable during testing: Proceed with installation

Demarcation Box cable retention/sealing: DexTidy cable retention system

Method to split grommets: Use any sharp blade

Tool for removing copper provisioning knockout: Flathead screwdriver

Site Investigation and Risk Assessment

Purpose of site investigation: Ensure plans are current

Responsibility for providing up to date plans: Asset owner

Features of site specific risk assessments: Identified hazards, control measures

Highest risk during detection on public highway: Injury from road traffic

Excavation and Backfilling

Safety measure for excavation near highway: Traffic control methods

Protective equipment for working outside highway space: High visibility clothing

Minimum clearance for works vehicle on 30mph road: 2m

Procedure for handling excavated materials: Segregate, cover, bund

Removal of unsuitable backfill materials: To licensed recycling site
