WHO WE ARE

WHO WE ARE: DexGreen, founded in 1994, develops innovative products for carrier networks worldwide, offering ongoing support and utilizing the latest AR and AI technology.

WHAT WE DO

WHAT WE DO: DexGreen manufactures over 200 unique telecom products, providing ongoing product support services through technical, commercial, and collaborative relationships.

INNOVATIVE AND COST-EFFECTIVE

INNOVATIVE AND COST-EFFECTIVE: DexGreen creates innovative and cost-effective telecom products to upgrade carrier networks, accredited to quality and environmental standards.

ACREDITATIONS

ACREDITATIONS: DexGreen holds accreditation to BS EN ISO9001:2015 and BS EN ISO14001:2015 standards for design, manufacture, and distribution of telecom products and services.

MISSION

MISSION: DexGreen's mission is to improve network health with innovative products, delivering cost savings through innovation, fault reduction, and supply chain refinement.

SUSTAINABILITY

SUSTAINABILITY: DexGreen is committed to sustainability, reducing single-use plastics, improving resource efficiency, and minimizing environmental impact through various initiatives.

FTTH – PRODUCT SOLUTIONS

FTTH – PRODUCT SOLUTIONS: Various product solutions for Fibre to the Home installations including exchange racks, optical distribution frames, fibre jumper management, optical fibre patch cords.

EXCHANGE

EXCHANGE: Information about optical distribution frames, fibre jumper management, exchange racks, and different types of optical fibre patch cords for exchange applications.

FIBRE TO THE HOME

FIBRE TO THE HOME: Details about optical fibre nodes, various types of ports, ribbon and hybrid splice trays, and different sizes of fibre optic cables suitable for home installations.

OPTICAL FIBRE PATCH CORDS

OPTICAL FIBRE PATCH CORDS: Overview of different types of optical fibre patch cords, connector variations, and configurations available for installations.

AERIAL NETWORK

AERIAL NETWORK: Introduction to slack management brackets, pole brackets, and clamps for aerial network installations to maintain proper cable routing and management.
