Foundational Knowledge: Introduction to DexGreen, its history since 1994, product development, manufacturing over 200 unique products, and services offered including a mobile learning platform for engineers.

Telecommunications Connectivity Products: Detailing of DexGreen's range of products designed for upgrading carrier networks, including over 200 unique telecommunications products.

Product Installation and Support: Explanation of ongoing support offered through AR and AI technology for digital installation assistance, alongside a mobile learning platform for on-the-job upskilling.

Environmental Sustainability: Insights into DexGreen's commitments to sustainability, including the reduction of single-use plastics, recycling initiatives, and adherence to environmental standards like BS EN ISO14001:2015.

Product Solutions for FTTH Networks: Comprehensive breakdown of DexGreen's fibre to the home (FTTH) solutions categorized by exchange, underground, aerial, SMDU/Façade, SDU, and MDU, including products for backbone fibre, distribution points, cables and accessories, demarcation/point of entry, customer lead-in cables, and fibre termination outlets.

Copper Access and Legacy Products: Overview of tools and accessories provided by DexGreen for copper connectivity, ensuring continued support for legacy telecommunications infrastructure.

Mobile App-Based Training: Information about the DexGreen mobile app that provides a learning platform for engineers, including interactive modules like videos, quizzes, and checklists for onsite upskilling.

AR and AI Digital Installation Assistance: Introduction to DexGreen’s innovative use of augmented reality (AR) and artificial intelligence (AI) to provide digital assistance during product installation, significantly reducing revisit costs.

Innovative and Cost-Effective Telecom Solutions: Insight into DexGreen’s approach to creating innovative and cost-effective solutions for the telecommunications industry, including ongoing product support and collaborative partnerships.

Quality and Environmental Standards: DexGreen's commitment to quality and environmental standards, evidenced by its accreditations to BS EN ISO9001:2015 and ISO14001:2015, encompassing design, manufacture, and distribution.

Network Health Improvement Mission: DexGreen's mission to enhance network health through innovative products, fault volume reduction, and supply chain refinement, with a focus on improving telecommunications infrastructure.

Sustainability Commitments: Details on DexGreen's sustainability initiatives, such as reducing single-use plastics, increasing recycling, cutting down energy and water usage, and promoting resource efficiency.

Fibre to the Home (FTTH) Solutions: In-depth look at DexGreen's FTTH solutions, including product options for exchange, underground, aerial, SMDU/Façade, SDU, and MDU applications.
