Who We Are: DexGreen is a leading manufacturer specializing in telecommunication connectivity solutions. Founded in 1994, the company has been at the forefront of developing innovative products that enhance carrier networks worldwide. DexGreen offers over 200 unique products along with digital tools like AR and AI technologies for efficient installations and mobile learning platforms for on-site upskilling.

What We Do: DexGreen focuses on creating innovative and cost-effective telecom products. With a commitment to quality and environmental standards, the company holds BS EN ISO9001:2015 and BS EN ISO14001:2015 accreditations. DexGreen aims to refine supply chains while improving network health through fault volume reduction and innovation.

Our Mission: DexGreen is dedicated to improving telecommunications infrastructure by innovating reliable and long-lasting components. The mission includes delivering whole life cost savings and being industry leaders in optical fibre and copper connectivity.

Sustainability: DexGreen prioritizes sustainability by minimizing paper usage through digital documentation, eliminating single-use plastics in packaging, recycling, and reducing water and energy usage. The company's environmental efforts align with the ISO14001 standard, emphasizing efficient environmental management and impact improvement.
