WHO WE ARE

WHO WE ARE: DexGreen, founded in 1994, develops innovative and tailor-made products to upgrade carrier networks worldwide. They manufacture over 200 unique telecom products and provide ongoing support through their mobile app.

WHAT WE DO

WHAT WE DO: DexGreen creates innovative and cost-effective telecom products, offering ongoing product support services through technical, commercial, and collaborative relationships with business partners.

INNOVATIVE AND COST-EFFECTIVE

INNOVATIVE AND COST-EFFECTIVE: DexGreen is dedicated to improving network health with innovative solutions that deliver whole life cost savings, fault volume reduction, and supply chain refinement. They are industry leaders in optical fibre and copper connectivity.

ACCREDITATIONS

ACCREDITATIONS: DexGreen is accredited to BS EN ISO9001:2015 quality standard and BS EN ISO14001:2015 environmental standard, covering design, manufacture, and distribution of products and services to various industries.

OUR MISSION

OUR MISSION: DexGreen's mission is to improve network health with products and solutions that deliver cost savings through innovation, fault reduction, and supply chain refinement, dedicated to improving telecommunications infrastructure.

SUSTAINABILITY

SUSTAINABILITY: DexGreen is committed to sustainability, reducing single-use plastics, waste, and environmental impact. They focus on reducing waste, energy usage, implementing recycling initiatives, and reducing air travel.

FIBRE TO THE HOME

1. OPTICAL DISTRIBUTION FRAME (ODF): DexGreen offers a variety of ODF solutions for fibre to the home networks, including high-density patch panels, splice trays, and management systems.

2. FIBRE JUMPER MANAGEMENT: The innovative fibre jumper management solutions by DexGreen provide efficient organisation and routing of fibres in exchange setups, improving network reliability and performance.

3. EXCHANGE RACK: The exchange racks by DexGreen offer secure and efficient mounting solutions for ODF equipment, ensuring proper organisation and accessibility in fibre networks.

4. OPTICAL FIBRE PATCH CORDS: DexGreen provides high-quality optical fibre patch cords for reliable and fast connections, ensuring seamless data transmission in fibre optic networks.
