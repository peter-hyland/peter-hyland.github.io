How to Create an Account: Email info@skillsbase.io to have an account created. You will receive login details after the account creation. Operator account credentials are separate from the SkillsBase App.

How to Sign In: Navigate to https://operator.skillsbase.io/ and enter the provided login details.

Changing your Password: Recommended to change your password upon first login through the Dashboard's 'My Account' section.

Using the Dashboard

Home Screen: Quick navigation and unique statistics.

Sidebar menu: Access to Courses, Users, Products, Resources, and more.

User Management

Viewing users: Filter, search, and export/import user lists. Create, edit, or delete user accounts and view their course progress.

Creating Users: Enter Name and Email, assign to Group, activate the account, and decide if the user should be an assessor.

Setting a Usersâ€™ Password: Option to manually create user passwords with specific requirements.

Assigning Courses to a User: Assign courses through the 'Edit user' page.

Viewing Users Training Course Progress: Track individual or overall progress, reset progressions, and download quiz results.

Course Management

Courses Overview: Organization of courses into modules and submodules. Features to create courses, assign links, and manage course settings.

Course Blocks

Labels: Indicators for default courses, publication status, and activity status.

Functions: Duplicate, delete courses, setup progression and access settings.

Creating Modules: Notify updates, enter title and description, set custom unlock dates, and manage status.

Creating Submodules: Selection of templates, input of key data, customization of progression settings.

Submodule Templates

List of Submodule Types: Variety of templates like Title Slide, Video Player, Mini Quiz, Checklist, Document Viewer, etc. for versatile content delivery.

Creating Content: Specify details like title, description, media, question types and conditions for each submodule type.

Troubleshooting

Common Issues: Solutions for login problems, forgotten passwords, push notification issues, team member addition, incorrect email registrations.

Contact: For unresolved issues, contact Mark Horgan at info@skillsbase.io.
