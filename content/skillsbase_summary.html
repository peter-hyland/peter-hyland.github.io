1. How to Create an Account

Details: To sign into the Operator Dashboard (Expert), contact info@skillsbase.io to have an account registered in your name.

Note: Operator account credentials cannot be used for the SkillsBase App.

2. How to Sign In

Details: Open the web browser and go to https://operator.skillsbase.io/ to log in using the provided credentials.

3. Changing your Password

Details: Navigate to 'My Account' and click 'Change Password' to update your password.
