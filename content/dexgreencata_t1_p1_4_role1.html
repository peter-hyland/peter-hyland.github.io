Introduction to DexGreen Products and Solutions

About DexGreen: Founded in 1994, DexGreen focuses on creating innovative and cost-effective products for telecommunications connectivity, offering a range of over 200 products.

Accreditations: ISO9001:2015 and ISO14001:2015 certified, covering design, manufacture, and distribution tailored to telecommunications and pharmaceutical industries.

Mission: Aim to improve network health through innovation, fault volume reduction, and supply chain refinement.

Sustainability: Commits to environmental sustainability by reducing paper usage, single-use plastics, and implementing recycling and energy-saving measures.

MDU Solutions (Multi-Dwelling Unit)

Building Entrance Point Solutions: Offering various sizes and capacities for fibre optic distribution frames and modular solutions.

Backbone Fibre: Features multicore backbone cables ranging from 12f to 144f, emphasizing durability and compliance.

Distribution Points: Universal solutions adaptable for multiple configurations and easy integration.

Distribution Cable and Accessories: Includes discreet, durable distribution cables and innovative accessories for efficient installation.

Demarcation / Point of Entry: Provides secure and easily adaptable demarcation boxes and wall entry covers.

Customer Lead In Cable: Flexible and robust options for connecting the outer network to the subscriber’s premises.

Fibre Termination Outlet: Versatile internal termination solutions, from compact to complete units, designed for ease and efficiency.

SDU Solutions (Single Dwelling Unit)

Distribution Points: Compact and efficient solutions suitable for SDU environments, supporting both splicing and pre-connectorized options.

Drop Cables: High-quality drop cables designed for flexibility and reliability in SDU applications.

Aerial Clamps and Brackets: Innovative clamping and bracket solutions for supporting aerial drop cables securely.

Demarcation and Entry Points: External demarcation solutions and wall entry covers for seamless transition into the subscriber’s home.

Customer Lead In Cable: Engineered for challenging installations, ensuring optimal performance and durability.

Fibre Termination Unit: Sleek and user-friendly units for terminating fibre in single dwelling units, ensuring subscriber satisfaction.

Product Solutions for Telecommunications Connectivity

Fibre to The Home (FTTH) Solutions: Comprehensive solutions covering exchange, underground, aerial, SMDU/Facade, SDU, and MDU aspects for fibre deployment.

Copper Access + Legacy Products: Maintains a line of tools and accessories supporting legacy copper network maintenance and connectivity.

Fibre Connectivity and Management

Optical Distribution Frame (ODF): High-density solutions enabling efficient cable management and easy access for maintenance and upgrades.

Fibre Jumper Management: Efficient systems for managing fibre jumpers, ensuring organized cabling and ease of access.

Aerial Network Solutions

Distribution Points: Aerial solutions including small and medium nodes, harnessing UV stabilized components suitable for aerial deployment.

Slack Management: Advanced slack management solutions for aerial setups, ensuring fibre protection and efficient cable management.

Underground Network Solutions

Optical Nodes: Variety of nodes suitable for underground deployment, ensuring resilience and long-term performance.

Metalwork and Subduct Accessories: Robust metalwork solutions and accessories facilitating secure underground fibre optic network installations.

Sustainability Initiatives

Environmental Standards: Focus on improving resource efficiency, reducing waste, and enhancing overall environmental impact.

Packaging and Recycling: Commitment to eliminating single-use plastics and implementing comprehensive recycling initiatives to minimize environmental footprint.
