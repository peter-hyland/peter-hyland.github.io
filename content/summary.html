WHO WE ARE

WHO WE ARE

  • DexGreen is a company founded in 1994 that develops innovative and tailor-made products to upgrade carrier networks across the globe. They manufacture over 200 unique products and provide ongoing support through their mobile app.

WHAT WE DO

WHAT WE DO

  • DexGreen is dedicated to improving telecommunications infrastructure by delivering cost-effective solutions that reduce fault volume and improve supply chain refinement. They are industry leaders in optical fibre and copper connectivity.

INNOVATIVE AND COST-EFFECTIVE

INNOVATIVE AND COST-EFFECTIVE

  • The team at DexGreen focuses on creating innovative and cost-effective telecom products. They provide ongoing support services through technical, commercial, and collaborative relationships with their partners.

ACCREDITATIONS

ACCREDITATIONS

  • DexGreen is accredited to BS EN ISO9001:2015 quality standard and BS EN ISO14001:2015 environmental standard. Their scope of approval covers design, manufacture, and distribution of products and services to various industries including telecommunications, pharmaceutical, and process industries.

SUSTAINABILITY

SUSTAINABILITY

  • DexGreen is committed to sustainability with accreditations to ISO9001:2015 and ISO14001:2015 standards. They focus on reducing single-use plastics in packaging, increasing recycling, reducing waste going to landfill, and implementing energy-saving initiatives.

FTTH – Product Solutions

FTTH – Product Solutions

  • DexGreen provides a range of product solutions for Fibre to the Home installations, including Exchange, Underground, Aerial, SMDU/Facade, SDU, MDU, Copper Access, Legacy Products, Tools & Accessories.

EXCHANGE

EXCHANGE

  • DexGreen offers Optical Distribution Frame (ODF) solutions with various options like Patch Panels, Rack Systems, Fiber Jumper Management, and Splitters for exchange installations. They focus on high-density, high-capacity solutions for network connectivity.

FIBRE JUMPER MANAGEMENT

FIBRE JUMPER MANAGEMENT

  • DexGreen provides innovative solutions for managing fibre jumpers in exchange environments. They offer a range of panels, trays, and accessories to ensure efficient routing, storage, and organization of fibre cables.

EXCHANGE RACK

EXCHANGE RACK

  • DexGreen offers Exchange Rack solutions for mounting ODF equipment into cabinets. The rack systems are designed for easy installation and use, with features like strain relief plates, mounting brackets, and cable management options.

OPTICAL FIBRE PATCH CORDS

OPTICAL FIBRE PATCH CORDS

  • DexGreen manufactures a variety of Optical Fibre Patch Cords for network installations. They provide options like LC/APC to LA/APC cables with different specifications and connectors to suit various installation requirements.

OPTICAL FIBRE CABLES

OPTICAL FIBRE CABLES

  • DexGreen produces Optical Fibre Cables for underground and aerial applications. They offer diverse options like Multicore cables, Blown Fibre cables, and Pulling Fibre cables with different fibre counts and materials for durability and performance.

EXCHANGE BASKET TRAYS

EXCHANGE BASKET TRAYS

  • DexGreen manufactures Exchange Basket Trays for organizing and managing cables in exchange installations. These trays ensure proper routing, support, and protection for optical fibre cables, contributing to efficient network operations.

SLACK MANAGEMENT BRACKETS

SLACK MANAGEMENT BRACKETS

  • DexGreen provides Slack Management Brackets for organizing and storing excess cable slack in aerial and underground installations. These brackets offer solutions for cable retention, overlength storage, and efficient deployment on poles and structures.

POLE BRACKETS

POLE BRACKETS

  • DexGreen offers Pole Brackets for aerial network deployments, including Multi-Purpose Pole Brackets, Aerial Pole Extenders, and Universal Pole Brackets. These brackets provide versatile mounting options for network equipment on poles and structures.

CLAMPS

CLAMPS

  • DexGreen manufactures various clamps and mounting accessories for securing equipment on poles and structures in aerial networks. These clamps offer reliable and robust solutions for attaching network components in outdoor environments.
