How to Sign In

Steps

  • Enter login details received via email

Troubleshooting

Issues

I can't sign into the dashboard.: Check URL and account creation

I have forgotten my password.: Reset on sign-in page

My users are having trouble logging in.: Resend sign-up info

Push Notifications are not sending.: Check notification settings

How do I add a team member?: Use Team Management page

A user registered with an incorrect email, how do I change it?: Edit user details

My problem is not listed.: Contact Mark Horgan
