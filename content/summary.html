How to Create an Account

To sign into the Operator Dashboard, email info@skillsbase.io to have an account created in your name. Your operator account credentials cannot be used for the SkillsBase App.

How to Sign In

Visit https://operator.skillsbase.io/ and enter the login details received via email.

Changing your Password

Change your password by accessing the Dashboard, clicking 'My Account', then 'Change Password'.

Using the Dashboard

Dashboard provides quick view options for Users, Groups, Courses, Modules, Products, and Resources. Sidebar menu allows access to various management options.

User Management

View, create, edit, and delete user accounts. Filter and export user data. Assign courses to users and view training course progress.

Course Management

Create, edit, and delete courses. Assign links, set course specifics, and manage course blocks and modules.

Submodule Templates

Includes various templates like Title Slide, Web Viewer, Video Player, Image Gallery, Itemised List, Do and Don'ts Scenarios, Mini Quiz, Step-by-Step Video Guide, Hotspot Image, Simple Question, Hotspot Uploader, Question Bank Quiz, Upload Media, Checklist, Document Viewer.

Troubleshooting

Addresses common issues like sign-in problems, password reset, user login trouble, push notification errors, team member addition, email change for users.
