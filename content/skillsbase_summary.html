CSP Installation

Unique Feature of CSP Box Lid: Locks at 45

Primary Function of CSP Demarcation Box: For installation of copper connections

Sealing of CSP after installation: Close the lid

Mounting the CSP to the customer's wall: Using glue

List of tools needed for installation: Use any tools available

Cable suitable for use outside premises: Outer sheathing is UV protected

Inside/Out Cable

Suitable Layer for use outside: Outer sheathing is UV protected

Equipment for managing cable bend radius: Inside/Out Cable

Measurement of cable length needed: From CSP to ONT only

Storage of cable slack in CSP: Inner slack storage

Stripping Inside/Out Fibre Cable: Open-edged blade

Action if fault found in cable during testing: Proceed with installation

Site Investigation

Purpose of site investigation: Ensure plans are current

Action at planning stage to ensure current plans: Carry out a site investigation

Request for buried services information: Provide up to date readable plans

Responsible for providing plans of known buried services: Asset owner

Checking to ensure plans are current: Date of issue

Underground Services

Location marking importance: Reduce risk of damage

Action to reduce risk post successful survey: Mark position of services

Services traced and marked throughout site: Note line changes

Identification of service colors: Yellow (gas), Blue (water), Orange (electricity)

Risk Assessment

Purpose of site-specific risk assessments: Identify hazards and risks

Risk rating calculation method: Likelihood x severity

PP3 to reduce road traffic risk: High visibility clothing

Control measure selection meaning: Balanced against risk level and practicality

Detection Equipment

PUWER regulation responsibility: Employer for equipment suitability

Testing and recalibration frequency: Dependent on manufacturer's instructions

Personal protective equipment selection: Site specific risk assessment

Equipment suitability at workplace: Ensure it is suitable for intended use

Traffic Management

Purpose of traffic management layout: Based on site survey risks

Equipment for vehicle and pedestrian safety: Footway boards, ramps

Minimum clearance for works vehicle on road: 5 metres (40mph), 2m (30mph)

Excavation Safety

Safety measures near excavation areas: Complete risk assessment

Safety zone width for gas pipes near excavators: 500mm

Safety measures for traffic near excavations: Traffic control methods

Protecting exposed water pipes in cold weather: Insulation with pipe lagging

Reinstatement Materials

Reuse of excavated materials: Encouraged for sustainability

Segregation of reusable materials: To prevent contamination

Preventing excessive dryness of materials: Keep covered

Handling of unsuitable materials: Stockpile separately, removal to recycling site
